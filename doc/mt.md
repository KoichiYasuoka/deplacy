## minn [Stanza](https://stanfordnlp.github.io/stanza)

```py
>>> import stanza
>>> nlp=stanza.Pipeline("mt")
>>> doc=nlp("Alla ħares ma kienx Majsi illi jagħmel ftit tad-dija.")
```
